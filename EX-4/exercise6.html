Lao She Teahouse is one exciting cultural place in Montreal derived from Beijing China. It is named after the drama Teahouse by Chinese author, Lao She. Lao She (1899-1966), real name Shu Qingchun, was of Manchurian descent and the famous author of the book called “Camel Xiangzi” also known in the US as “Rickshaw”. Lao She Tea House was named after Mr. Lao She, where provides an antique-flavor,Beijing-styled environment, where you can listen wonderful Beijing Opera by celebrities from folk arts and drama on any given day while enjoying famous tea, palace snacks as well as traditional Beijing flavor state.
